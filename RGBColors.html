Shades of gray

By using equal values for red, green, and blue, you will get different shades of gray:

rgb(60, 60, 60)

rgb(90, 90, 90)

rgb(120, 120, 120)

rgb(180, 180, 180)

rgb(210, 210, 210)

rgb(240, 240, 240)

HSL Lightness

The third parameter of hsl() defines the lightness. 0% means black, and 100% means white:

hsl(0, 100%, 0%)

hsl(0, 100%, 25%)

hsl(0, 100%, 50%)

hsl(0, 100%, 75%)

hsl(0, 100%, 90%)

hsl(0, 100%, 100%)
